HOTEL ROOMS & SUITES
Somanya Royal Hotel is ideal for travelers visiting Somanya for short city breaks as well as long stays.

Modern décor, attention to detail and unrivaled luxury are all staples of the rooms and suites in this hotel. Dressed in neutral tones and complemented by Art Deco lines, works of art and contrasting materials, including tiled floors, each luxury room brings French charm to life.
